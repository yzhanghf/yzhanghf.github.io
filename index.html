<h3>About me</h3>
<p>I received my PhD degree at the <a href="http://www.stat.lsa.umich.edu/", style="text-decoration: none">Department of Statistics</a> at the University of Michigan.
I received my Bachelor of Science degree in Statistics at <a href="http://en.ustc.edu.cn/", style="text-decoration: none">University of Science and Technology of China</a>.</p>
<p>My co-advisors are Professor <a href="http://www.stat.lsa.umich.edu/~elevina/", style="text-decoration: none">Elizaveta Levina</a> and Professor <a href="http://www.stat.lsa.umich.edu/~jizhu/", style="text-decoration: none">Ji Zhu</a>.</p>
<p>Fall 2016: I joined <a href="http://www.stat.osu.edu/", style="text-decoration: none">Department of Statistics</a>, Ohio State University as an assistant professor.</p>
